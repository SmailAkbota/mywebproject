Countries I Visited

Korea

Korea is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich culture, and modern technology. I visited Seoul, where I enjoyed the traditional palaces, street food, and vibrant nightlife. The mix of ancient temples and modern skyscrapers was unforgettable.

Egypt's ancient history was the highlight of my visit. I saw the Great Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, which were awe-inspiring. I also took a cruise down the Nile River, learning about the fascinating culture and history of the Pharaohs.
